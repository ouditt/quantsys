# SKILL: Scaling Roadmap (cards 2+12) — Strategy Engineer, at milestones
GOAL: grow £500-1500 toward target WITHOUT breaking the statistical edge.

RULES (from sizing.scaling_roadmap):
1. Risk PERCENT never changes with equity; the £ risk unit grows because
   equity grows. Milestones are 1.5x equity steps (500->750->1125->...).
2. GATE to step up a rung: >=30 trades at current rung AND max DD < 15% AND
   live expectancy within 1 SE of certified. All three or stay.
3. On a 10% drawdown: drop one rung until a NEW equity high.
4. Venue floors: at <£2k, FX micro-lots and CFD minimums force oversizing —
   the roadmap keeps small accounts in crypto + fractional shares until
   ~£3-5k. Never clear a venue floor by raising risk.
5. THE mistake that permanently turns winners into losers: stepping size up
   after a hot streak, so the normal drawdown that follows lands on the
   largest size ever carried. Steps are capped at 1.5x and NEVER follow
   streaks — only milestones + gates.
6. Cadence honesty: daily bars ≈ 25-40 flagship trades/yr. "Quickly" is not a
   property of the edge; it's a property of risk, and its price is the
   P(DD>30%) column in `python -m qtsys.sizing`. Quote that table whenever
   asked to go faster.
